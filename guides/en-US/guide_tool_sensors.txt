The Sensors tool can be used to test the sensors on your device.

## Sensor list
The sensor list shows all the sensors available on your device that Trail Sense uses. If the accuracy of the sensor is available, it will be shown on the left, and the sensor's value will appear to the right of that.

## All sensors
To see all available sensors on your device, click the button in the top-right corner of the screen. This will show a list of all sensors available on your device, including those that Trail Sense does not use.

## Quick action
You can enable the Recalibrate Altimeter quick action in the settings for the desired tab.

To use the quick action, tap the **Recalibrate Altimeter** button. What happens depends on your current altimeter mode:

- **Barometer or Manual**: A dialog will appear where you can enter your current elevation. Once confirmed, the altimeter calibration will be updated.
- **GPS + Barometer or DEM + Barometer**: Trail Sense will immediately read your GPS and barometer to recalibrate the altimeter baseline. A loading indicator will appear while the recalibration is in progress.
- **GPS or DEM**: Opens the altimeter calibration settings.

Long-pressing the quick action always opens the altimeter calibration settings.
