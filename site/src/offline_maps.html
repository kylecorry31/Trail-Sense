Offline Maps

Trail Maps

Trail Sense supports Mapsforge maps. Here are some places to find them:

  • Mapsforge Download Server: https://download.mapsforge.org/maps/v5/
  • Mirror Rechenzentrum der Hochschule Esslingen, University of Applied Sciences: https://ftp-stud.hs-esslingen.de/pub/Mirrors/download.mapsforge.org/maps/v5/

Photo Maps

Trail Sense supports Geospatial PDFs. Here are some places to find them:

  • Caltopo: https://caltopo.com
  • QGIS: https://qgis.org (advanced, requires installing on a computer)
