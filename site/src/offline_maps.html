Offline Maps

Trail Sense supports Mapsforge maps. You can find maps through the following sources:

  • Mapsforge Download Server: https://download.mapsforge.org/

Trail Sense also supports Geospatial PDFs. The following sources are recommended:

  • Caltopo: https://caltopo.com
  • QGIS: https://qgis.org (advanced, requires installing on a computer)
