Trail Sense: Wilderness Survival Guide

A practical survival guide for hikers, backpackers, and campers. This guide covers the essentials of what to do in a survival situation, signaling for help, building a shelter, insulating clothing, finding food and water, starting a fire, and predicting the weather. This guide purposely excludes advanced bushcraft and long term survival skills in an effort to be as practical to the average person as possible.

Buying the paperback helps support Trail Sense's development.


This guide provides general educational information only. It is not professional training, medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment, and it does not replace emergency services, qualified healthcare providers, local guidance, or product/manufacturer instructions. In an emergency, contact emergency services as soon as possible, follow their instructions, and do not delay seeking help to consult this guide. Do not rely on this guide, Trail Sense, or your phone as your only safety plan or means of communication. Conditions, risks, laws, and recommended practices vary by location and situation; verify information before acting and use your own judgment.
