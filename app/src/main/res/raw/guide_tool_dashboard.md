The Dashboard displays all of your selected in-app widgets in one place, giving you a quick overview of important information.

## Viewing widgets
When you open the Dashboard, it shows all widgets you have enabled under Settings > Tools > In-app widgets. The same widgets appear here as in the widgets tab of the quick action sheet.

## Configuring widgets
To change which widgets are displayed, go to Settings > Tools > In-app widgets and select the widgets you want to show.
