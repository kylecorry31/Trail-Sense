The Survival Guide tool can be used to reference information about wilderness survival.

## Usage
Open the Survival Guide tool and select which chapter you would like to read. Tap the section in the chapter to expand the content.

## Search
You can search for content in the survival guide using the search bar at the top of the screen. You will see the most relevant section's content displayed at the top of the screen and a list of search results at the bottom with brief summaries. Tapping any of these results will open the chapter with the section expanded.

## Quick action
You can enable the Survival Guide quick action in the settings for the tab you want it on.

To use the quick action, tap the quick action button to open a search box. The most relevant section's content will be displayed. Tapping on the section will open the chapter with the section expanded. Tapping the guide icon in the top right of the sheet will open the Survival Guide tool with your search query.
